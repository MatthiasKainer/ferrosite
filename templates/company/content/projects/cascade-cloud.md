## Situation

Cascade Cloud was growing quickly, but its self-serve roots were colliding
with an upmarket sales motion. Enterprise buyers landed on pages built for a
very different audience and stalled before reaching the right team.

## What changed

We redesigned the site architecture around buyer intent, clarified packaging
language, and mapped the experience into CRM and lifecycle workflows so the
new positioning continued after the first visit.

## Outcome

- clearer qualification for enterprise leads
- better alignment between pricing, product marketing, and sales
- a delivery stack that supported iteration instead of brittle one-off pages
