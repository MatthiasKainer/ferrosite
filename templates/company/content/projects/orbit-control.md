## Situation

Orbit Control had a strong product and a weak market story. Buyers could not
tell whether the company sold software, consulting, or both. Sales decks,
website copy, and onboarding materials all framed the value differently.

## What changed

We rebuilt the narrative around a single operating promise, then translated it
into a modular web experience with clearer entry points for technical buyers,
financial stakeholders, and implementation teams.

## Outcome

- faster qualification because buyers self-selected into the correct path
- cleaner handoff from marketing to sales
- a reusable messaging system the internal team could extend without another
  full redesign
