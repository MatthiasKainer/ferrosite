## Situation

The client wanted faster service coordination and better use of historical job
knowledge, but the first wave of AI prototypes created more confusion than
confidence. Teams were unsure when to trust the output and when to override it.

## What changed

We designed an operator-facing workflow: retrieval sources were constrained,
handoff rules were explicit, and every automation step had a human checkpoint
for high-risk actions.

## Outcome

- faster access to prior job knowledge
- higher confidence because the decision boundaries were visible
- a rollout plan leadership could approve without betting the business on a
  black box
